      About the Program

      The MISNS Advocacy & Ambassador Program advances the mission of the Moral Injury Support Network for Servicewomen (MISNS) by raising awareness of moral injury and improving how systems respond to women veterans experiencing it.

      Our advocacy is educational, relationship-driven, and survivor-centered‚Äînot confrontational or political lobbying. We combine lived experience, service outcomes, and real community needs to influence policies, procedures, and access pathways across nonprofit, government, and corporate systems.
