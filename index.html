      About the Veterans Hope Project

      The Veterans Hope Project, led by the Moral Injury Support Network for Servicewomen (MISNS), is dedicated to supporting women veterans and their families facing challenges such as PTSD, economic hardship, and social isolation. Over the next year, our goal is to serve over 1,200 women and families by providing essential resources like food, clothing, transportation, and mental health support.
